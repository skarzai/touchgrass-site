# Privacy Policy — TouchGrass

**Effective date:** April 17, 2026
**Last updated:** April 17, 2026

TouchGrass ("the app," "we," "our," or "us") is built on a simple premise: your screen time data is personal, and it should never leave your device.

This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses, how it's used, and — importantly — what we do *not* do with it.

---

## Summary, in plain English

- **We don't collect any personal data.** There are no accounts, no logins, no servers.
- **We don't track you.** No analytics, no fingerprinting, no ad IDs.
- **We don't share anything.** Your screen time, your app usage, your habits — all of it stays on your iPhone.
- **We don't see your data.** We *can't*, because it never leaves your device.

---

## What data the app accesses

TouchGrass uses Apple's **Family Controls**, **DeviceActivity**, and **ManagedSettings** frameworks to function. These are Apple-provided APIs that run on your device. When you grant permission, the app can:

- Read the total time you've spent on your iPhone (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Read which apps and app categories you've used and for how long
- Count your pickups (how many times you unlocked your phone)
- Block or shield specific apps you choose when you hit a limit you set

All of this information is processed **locally, on your device, by Apple's own frameworks**. The app itself never sees raw per-app usage data in a way that could leave the device.

---

## What we store on your device

The following is stored on your iPhone only, using Apple's secure on-device storage (MMKV + SQLite):

- Your daily screen time goal
- Your app usage totals (aggregated per day)
- Your garden state (growth stage, water level, streak count)
- Your equipped accessories and customizations
- Your app limits and focus session history
- App preferences (theme, notifications, onboarding status)

You can wipe all of this at any time by going to **Maintain → Clear All Data**.

---

## What we don't do

We do **not**:
- Have a server or backend
- Send any data over the network (ever)
- Use third-party analytics services (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, nothing)
- Use third-party ad networks
- Use crash reporting services that send data off-device
- Access your contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera
- Share, sell, lease, or disclose any information to any third party
- Track your behavior across other apps or websites
- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

---

## Permissions we request

| Permission | Why |
|------------|-----|
| **Family Controls / Screen Time** | To read your screen time data so your garden can respond to it, and to block apps you choose to limit. |
| **Notifications** (optional) | To remind you to check on your garden and to alert you when you hit a daily limit. You can decline and the app still works. |

You can revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

---

## Children's privacy

TouchGrass does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+ and safe for all ages. Because no data is collected, transmitted, or stored off-device, the app is compliant with **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by design.
