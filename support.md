## 🐛 Common issues

**"The app can't see my screen time"**
- Go to iPhone Settings → Screen Time → make sure it's turned on
- In TouchGrass, go to Maintain → re-grant Screen Time permission

**"My app limits aren't blocking apps"**
- Family Controls needs to be re-authorized. Maintain → Reset Permissions → re-grant
- Confirm the app you're trying to block is actually in your limit list (Protect tab)

**"My Sproutly won't evolve"**
- Evolution requires hitting a minimum number of growth days at each stage
- Seed → Sprout: 2 days
- Sprout → Sapling: 5 days
- Sapling → Bloom: 12 days
- Bloom → Tree: 25 days
- The "Evolve" button appears on the Home screen only when you're ready

**"I want to start over"**
- Maintain → Clear All Data — wipes everything, local to your device only
